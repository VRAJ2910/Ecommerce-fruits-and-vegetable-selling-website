Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services.

How do I place an order?

You can place an order through our website or mobile app. Simply browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. We accept all major credit cards and digital payment methods.

What are your delivery options?

We offer same-day delivery for orders placed before 2 PM, next-day delivery, and scheduled delivery. Delivery fees vary based on your location and chosen delivery time.

Do you offer organic products?

Yes! All our products are certified organic and sourced from local farms. We work directly with farmers to ensure the highest quality and freshness.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with your order, contact us within 24 hours and we'll replace or refund your purchase.

How can I track my order?

You can track your order through our website or mobile app. You'll receive real-time updates via email and SMS, including delivery notifications.
